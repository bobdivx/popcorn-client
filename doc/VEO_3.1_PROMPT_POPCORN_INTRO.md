# Veo 3.1 Prompt: Popcorn Logo Animation

## Prompt for Veo 3.1

Create a cinematic logo animation similar to the iconic Netflix "N" animation, but featuring the letter "P" for Popcorn. The animation should embody a modern, premium streaming platform aesthetic with a dark, sophisticated color palette.

**Visual Style:**
- Deep charcoal black background (#121212, anthracite)
- Neon violet/purple primary color (#a855f7, vibrant purple)
- Glassmorphic effects with subtle transparency and blur
- Premium, sleek, and minimalist design
- Modern sans-serif typography (Inter/Roboto style, bold and geometric)

**Animation Sequence:**
1. Start with a dark, empty screen (anthracite background #121212)
2. The letter "P" materializes from the center in a bold, geometric sans-serif font
3. The "P" appears with a neon violet glow effect (#a855f7) that pulses gently
4. Subtle glassmorphic particles or light rays emanate from the "P" with a soft blur effect
5. The "P" scales up slightly (1.1x) with a smooth, elegant motion
6. A soft violet glow expands outward from the letter, creating a subtle ripple effect
7. The animation concludes with the "P" fully illuminated, glowing with neon violet light (rgba(168, 85, 247, 0.8))
8. Optional: Subtle popcorn particles or cinematic elements floating in the background

**Technical Details:**
- Duration: 3-5 seconds
- Smooth, cinematic camera movement (slight zoom in or subtle rotation)
- Professional lighting with neon violet accents
- Depth of field effects for premium look
- Smooth easing curves (cubic-bezier transitions)
- High contrast between dark background and bright violet letter
- Subtle motion blur for fluidity

**Color Palette:**
- Background: Deep charcoal/anthracite (#121212)
- Primary letter: White or light gray (#ffffff or #f5f5f5)
- Glow effect: Neon violet (#a855f7, rgba(168, 85, 247, 0.5-0.8))
- Accent particles: Subtle violet tints with transparency

**Typography:**
- Bold, geometric sans-serif letter "P"
- Similar to Inter or Roboto font family
- Clean, modern, and highly legible
- Letter should be substantial and prominent

**Mood & Atmosphere:**
- Premium streaming platform aesthetic
- Cinematic and sophisticated
- Modern and cutting-edge
- Elegant and refined
- Similar energy to Netflix intro but with unique Popcorn identity

**Reference Style:**
- Netflix "N" animation: smooth, iconic, memorable
- But with Popcorn's violet neon theme instead of red
- Dark, moody atmosphere with vibrant accent color
- Professional production quality

The letter "P" appears on a deep charcoal background (#121212) with a bold geometric sans-serif font.
